About Us

Hacker Wave is a tech club dedicated to advancing knowledge in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity. We aim to foster a collaborative environment where learning, innovation, and hands-on experience can thrive among tech enthusiasts.

Our Activities

  • Workshops & Seminars: Hands-on learning sessions covering the latest trends in AI, ML, and Cybersecurity.
  • Competitions & Hackathons: Competitive events promoting innovation and teamwork.
  • Collaborative Projects: Teamwork-driven initiatives to solve real-world challenges.
